Rogue Theater's New Location

Rogue Theater will have a permanent presence in Sturgeon Bay. After years of renting different locations, the local favorite is constructing a new facility on North 14th Avenue. The local play house has hosted many popular productions over the years. The whole community is excited to support their theatrical endeavors for many years to come.
