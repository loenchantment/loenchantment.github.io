Site Purpose

The purpose of the Sturgeon bay, WI Chamber site is to support the businesses in town, both small and large. It further seeks to connect potential customers directly to the entities that can fulfill their needs for goods or services while promoting the beauty of the community.

Logo

The logo was designed with the history of Sturgeon Bay in mind. Ships have been traveling to and from this beautiful town situated on Lake Michigan since 1850.
