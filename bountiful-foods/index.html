So Cal Spotlight: Solana Beach

We proudly serve the Southern California Coast. From the beaches to the hiking trails, our fresh fruit smoothies will give you energy for your explorations.

Solana Beach has a long history as a community. Situated on the north coast of San Diego County (only 30 minutes from downtown San Diego), this lovely community is full of seaside character. Founded in 1902 by Colonel Ed Fetcher who had the foresight to buy just over 200 acres from local farmer George H. Jones, Solana Beach has grown into a tourist destination with an artistic flair. The Cedros Design District features well-known designers. It may be the beauty of the location that inspires their work

Speaking of natural beauty, Solana Beach boasts beautiful beaches where you can stroll, surf, and soak in the sun. Tide Beach Park, Solana Beach County Park, and Fletcher Cove Beach Park are ready for you to explore.

While you're enjoying all that Solana Beach has to offer, we'd love to bring you a healthy fresh fruit drink to fuel the fun.
